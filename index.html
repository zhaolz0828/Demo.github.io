Overview

MRTC is a novel system designed for collaborative operations of multiple USVs in urban cleaning. It optimizes task assignment and path planning, reducing costs and improving efficiency.

Key Features

  • Dynamic Task Assignment with AC
  • Optimized Multi-USV Path Planning
  • Adaptation to Environmental Changes
  • Cost-effective Cleaning Operations
  • Multi-USV Collaborative Operations
  • Sustainable and Energy-efficient Solutions

Experimental Results

  • Operation cost reduced by 13.68%
  • Efficiency improved by 18% compared to SOTA
  • Total driving distance: 10,775 km/month
  • Energy savings: 20,575 kWh/month
  • Labor savings: 2,744 working hours/month
